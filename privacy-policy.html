Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2025

1. What We Collect

While using Soooots.com, we may collect two kinds of information:

  • Personal Data: This includes information you choose to provide—like your name or email—when subscribing, reaching out, or submitting a suggestion.

2. How We Use It

The data we collect helps us:

We may use cookies to remember your preferences and track general user behavior. These cookies are harmless and do not give us access to your device. You can disable cookies anytime through your browser settings.

Your information is safe with us. We don’t sell or rent your personal data. If we ever work with third-party tools (like email platforms or analytics), they are only allowed access to what’s necessary and must keep your data secure.

5. Outside Links

Occasionally, you may find links on Soooots.com that lead to other websites. Please note we aren’t responsible for how those sites handle your information. We recommend reviewing their privacy terms separately.

6. Security Measures

We follow reasonable steps to secure the data we handle, both technically and organizationally. While no system is immune to risks, we constantly monitor and update our safeguards.

7. Your Control

You’re in control of your data. If you want to update, remove, or review the personal info we may hold, just reach out. We’ll do our best to respond quickly and respectfully.

8. Children Under 13

Soooots.com is not made for children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect information from anyone in that age group. If a child has provided personal info, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

This Privacy Policy may evolve as our site grows or laws change. When that happens, we’ll post the updated version here along with the new effective date.
