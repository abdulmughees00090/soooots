Whether you're craving adventure or just want to escape your neighbor's karaoke, we’ve got ideas.

Step 1: Start with the Budget (Or Pretend You Have One)

Got cash? Great. Got none? Even better. Some of the best memories are made on a budget (or your friend's credit card).

Step 2: Solo or Squad?

Decide if you’re flying solo or dragging your indecisive friends along. Either way, make a plan and then ignore it completely.

Step 3: Local Vibes or Big Journeys

From local cafes to faraway places you can't pronounce — it's all a vibe. Just make sure it has good lighting for pictures.

Step 4: Outfit Inspo First, Destination Second

You picked that cute outfit — now find a place worthy of it. That’s the real order of decision-making here.

Step 5: Embrace the Unexpected

Detours? Yes. Lost GPS signal? Absolutely. That weird roadside attraction? A must-see.

Final Thoughts

It doesn’t matter where you go as long as you feel like you belong there… or at least look great pretending to.
