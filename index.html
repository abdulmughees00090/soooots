Summer Styles for Women: 2025 Edition

From lawn prints to flowy maxis, Pakistani summer fashion is all about light fabrics, floral motifs, and pastel tones. Brands like Khaadi, Gul Ahmed, and So Kamal are releasing their latest collections with modern cuts blended with cultural aesthetics.

Trendy Men's Wear This Season

Kurta trousers and minimal streetwear dominate this summer. Earth tones, lightweight fabrics, and subtle embroidery are making a big comeback in Pakistani menswear. Don't miss Sapphire Men's or Bonanza Satrangi's new drop.

Unisex Accessories That Elevate Your Look

Brown leather sandals, canvas totes, and sunglasses with tinted lenses are trending hard. Look out for handcrafted items from local artisans — they’re stylish and support small businesses!
