Home Who We Are Suggest A Confusion

So you’re staring at your closet like it owes you answers? Been there. Let’s style this confusion out of your life.

Step 1: Check the Weather (And Then Ignore It)

The forecast says sunny but your soul says hoodie. Who do you trust more? Exactly. Dress for the vibe, not just the temperature.

Step 2: What's the Occasion?

Going out for coffee? That’s runway. Meeting friends? That’s red carpet. Grocery shopping? Still red carpet. Every day is a fashion show if you squint hard enough.

Step 3: Mix Comfort with Chaos

That oversized tee with those sparkly pants? Yes. That sweatshirt over a flowy skirt? Bold. You’re not just dressing up, you’re starting trends.

Step 4: Don't Fight the Accessories

Sunglasses indoors? Iconic. Bucket hat at night? Revolutionary. That scarf you haven’t washed in weeks? Vintage, darling.

Step 5: Color Mood Check

Blue? Chill. Red? Drama. All black? Mystery. Tie-dye rainbow? Chaos energy. Let your colors scream before you even speak.

Step 6: Mirror Time

Stand in front of the mirror. Spin. Do finger guns. If you don’t feel like a main character, add one more thing.

Final Thoughts

Wear what makes you smile, even if it makes others squint. Clothes aren’t just fabric—they’re mood, message, and sometimes a warning. Now go out and turn sidewalks into catwalks.
